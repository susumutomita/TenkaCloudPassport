# Field Evaluation Research Consent Script (English)

The Facilitator reads the following before any Product interaction begins.

> This asks whether you want to join research about TenkaCloud Passport. It is separate from consent to use
> the Product. You may use the app without joining the research, and you may stop at any time without giving
> a reason.
>
> The research counts Start, Ready, Bridge or no-signal, the kind of Provider used, and an optional one-tap
> answer of “conversation started,” “not yet,” or “prefer not to answer.” These are aggregate counters held on
> this device. We do not collect names, device IDs, Lounge IDs, exact times, locations, Passport content,
> Bridge content, or conversation content. The Facilitator records setup and support only in broad time bands
> and does not write down what anyone says.
>
> The self-report is optional. You can finish immediately without answering. A missing answer is never counted
> as a conversation start. The on-device aggregate disappears when the app process ends. It can be shared only
> by a manual action after every field is previewed and at least five outcomes have been counted. Five outcomes
> reduce small-group inference; they do not guarantee anonymity.
>
> Before observations are combined, you can ask us to leave yours out. After an observation has been mixed into
> counters that do not identify individuals, we cannot tell which count was yours and cannot remove it separately.
>
> Do you agree to join this research? Refusing does not change your access to the Product.

Only include the interaction in research tallies after an explicit yes. Do not record a named response or create a
participant-level consent list. Enable the research counters only if everyone in the session consented; otherwise
leave them off while the Product remains available. The participant must separately confirm the Passport fields and
Owner Answer in the Product UI. Research consent never substitutes for Product consent.
