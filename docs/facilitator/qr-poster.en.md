# Scan the Current Lounge QR

- Kit Version: 1.0.

## 1. Check it yourself

Use only the fresh QR the Host is displaying in the app now for you alone. Never use a photograph, pre-printed
invite, or another group's QR. After successful authentication, that QR is used. The next person waits for the
Host to rotate the Secret and display a different fresh QR. Never let multiple people scan the same QR.

## 2. Review your own Preview

Choose camera permission and Passport fields yourself. The Facilitator never acts on your behalf.

## 3. Choose Ready or leave

Owner Question, Ready, research, and leaving are optional. You may stop when anything is unclear.

→ The current QR remains on the live device held by the Host. Never paste or print a QR on this poster.

Stop scanning when the app says the QR is expired, duplicate, used, invalid, unsupported, or for another group.
Never retry the same QR. Start again only if the intended Group Host can show a fresh current Invite. Never
photograph, forward, print, transcribe, or reuse the QR, and never type the secret.

Lounge-derived data is discarded by the earliest of leaving, Host end, or the 20-minute expiry. `no-signal` and
leaving are normal and are not person evaluations.
