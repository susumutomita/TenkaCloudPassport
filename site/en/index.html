How it works

All you hand over is one QR code.

  1. Create your Intro Card

    Enter your name (required), title, organization, self-introduction, and links. Nothing leaves your device until you explicitly save it.

  2. Show the QR code

    Once saved, your name, self-introduction, and a large, real QR code appear on screen. Just show this screen to the other person.

  3. They scan it with a standard camera

    No app install required. Scanning the QR with an iPhone or Android standard camera opens your Intro Card page in a browser (the other person needs to be online). Adding you to contacts is an optional action inside that page.

  4. The conversation continues

    After reading your self-introduction, the other person picks up the conversation from there. This replaces exchanging business cards with handing over a conversation starter.

Adding to contacts has been verified only on iPhone/Safari. On Android it requires one extra step—save the .vcf file, then open it—and remains unverified. In in-app browsers such as LINE, X, or Instagram, saving the file may fail entirely.

Privacy by design

Not holding your data is the design.

No account

No sign-up, no login. Your data never touches a server.

Everything stays on-device

Your Intro Card is stored only on your device. There is no export or backup feature.

Sharing is just showing a QR code

There is no path for receiving someone else's information. You only ever hand over your own card.

No telemetry

Nothing is collected or transmitted. The design requires no server and no account.

Try it

Run it yourself.

git clone https://github.com/susumutomita/TenkaCloudPassport.git
cd TenkaCloudPassport
make install
make start   # scan the QR from Expo Go on your phone

Install Expo Go on your phone, connect it to the same network as your Mac, then scan the QR code shown in the terminal. To try it in a browser instead, run bun run web.

No dev environment handy? Open the Web version directly in your phone's browser. Data is stored in the browser's on-device storage, so switching browsers or devices does not carry your card over, and the first load requires an online connection. On iOS Safari, tap the Share icon and choose "Add to Home Screen"; on Android Chrome, open the menu and choose "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app" to launch it from an icon on your home screen (this is not App Store or Play Store distribution).

Scanning this QR code with a physical device's standard camera and confirming it opens the Intro Card page in a browser is Not run. We do not call green CI physical-device evidence. See the README for details.
